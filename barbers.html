APPOINTMENTS

MANUALLY INPUT APPOINTMENTS

You can manually input single and recurring appointments for those clients who have scheduled with you outside of the app. If you provide the client's phone number, we will text them a confirmation of their booking.

CLIENT BOOKED APPOINTMENTS

Clients can book with you directly based on your availability.
BOOKING PREFERENCES
Define limits on last minute appointments. Choose the time intervals that clients can book. Control how your clients book recurring appointments. Turn on auto confirmations to spend more time cutting hair and less in the app.
REMINDERS
Clients that booked through the app will receive a reminder 2 hours before their appointments reducing the number of no-shows.

COMMENTS

Conversations with clients are usually regarding an appointment. Communicate with clients by commenting on an appointment keeping conversations organized and relevant.

SCHEDULE

CALENDAR

View all of your appointments in one place as they are scheduled. Spend less time worrying about your schedule. No more double bookings.

DAILY REMINDERS

Barbers receive a daily reminder of how many appointments are scheduled that day.

BREAKS & AVAILABILITY

Define any recurring breaks you may have in your schedule. Block out times for vacation and unexpected leave.

CLIENT MANAGEMENT

CLIENT NOTES

As a barber, it's tough to remember specific details about how to best service each and every client. Keep notes on all of your clients that you can quickly reference before each appointment.

CLIENT HISTORY

View your history of appointments with each client. Keep track of the time between their visit, and see who's due for cut.

CLIENT BLAST

Send important announcements to all of your clients at once. Let them know of any deals and promotions, or if you have to leave the shop for vacation or any other reason.

PROFILE

BRANDING

BUSINESS INFORMATION
Add your address, hours of availability, and services. Clients have all the information they need before booking their appointment with you.
PHOTOS
Images of your work speak much louder than words. Showcase your best cuts to show new clients why they should book with you.
RATINGS & REVIEWS
Let your clients brag about how nice you are. New clients are much more likely to book with barbers that have reviews.

CUSTOM PROFILE LINK

Create your own custom profile link that you can share with your clients and market yourself. Clicking this link will prompt the client to download the app. Once they have signed up, they will immediately be taken to your profile.

VISIBLE TO NEW CLIENTS

Once you’ve completed your profile, potential clients can discover you with no work on your end. Get new clients without stepping out of the shop.

Reduce the amount of cash you have to carry around. Send your payments directly to your bank account. Receive your payments as quick as next business day. 2.95% transaction fee applies.

IN-APP PAYMENTS

Allow clients to add a payment method when booking. The client is charged after the service once you've checked-out their appointment. We recommend doing this while the client is still in the chair.

Earn back lost revenue by charging clients for last minute cancellations and no-shows. Clients will be informed of any cancellation and no-show fees when they book.
